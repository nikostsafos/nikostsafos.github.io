Summary

A data-driven storyteller with experience in business, academia, non-profits and government. Have advised companies and governments in over 30 countries on some of the world’s most complex energy projects; lecturer at a top graduate school for international affairs; author of a book on the Greek crisis and dozens of articles on energy, economics, politics; fledging programmer—proficient in R, data analysis and visualization, and, less so, D3.js, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python.
