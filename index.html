About me

Data-driven storyteller. Have advised companies and governments in over 30 countries on the world’s most complex energy projects; lecturer at Johns Hopkins SAIS; author of a blog and a book on the Greek crisis, and articles in Foreign Affairs, the National Interest, and elsewhere; quoted in the Financial Times, the New York Times, Reuters, and other outlets; proficient in R, data analysis and visualization, and, less so, D3.js, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python.
